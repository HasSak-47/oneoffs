Getting Started with Notes

1. Creating Files/Folders:
   - Click the "+" icons next to folders to create new items
   - Give your file/folder a name and press Enter

2. Editing Notes:
   - Click on any file to open it in the editor
   - Make your changes - they save automatically
   - Click the checkmark to manually save

3. Organizing:
   - Create nested folders for better organization
   - Files and folders can be deleted when no longer needed

4. Mobile Use:
   - Tap the menu icon to show/hide the file explorer
   - The editor automatically adjusts to your screen size

Your notes are private and only stored in your browser. Enjoy!
